About our Tool

Our website acts as a cost of living comparison calculator. On a basic level, users can input their total income as well as the state they are from and the state they plan to move to. The site will calculate the cost of living comparison between the two states. Users will be shown a drop-down selection of their current state and the state they are comparing to.

Resources We will be basing our calculations on official government statistics that have already computed the composite cost of living index for Quarter 4 2020. This information was gathered from cities across the nation that participated in the Council for Community and Economic Research survey on a volunteer basis.
